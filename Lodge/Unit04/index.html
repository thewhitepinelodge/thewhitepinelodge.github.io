unit four

THIS UNIT IS CURRENTLY RENTED.

Unit Four is on the top floor of the lodge. The living room has a free-standing fireplace and a wood bin, two single beds/couches, a table with four chairs, a television with satellite access, and a VCR.

One bedroom has a double bed; the second bedroom has either two twin beds or one king-sized bed. The bathroom has a shower, and the kitchen is quite large. It includes a table with three chairs, a breakfast-hutch holding the dishes, a full-sized refrigerator with a separate freezer, and a full-sized stove. table that can open up to accommodate eight people. Unit Four also features its own thermostat and ample closet space.

The sloping ceilings give the apartment a cozy garret look. Six can sleep in comfort here. Click on an image to see a larger version.
