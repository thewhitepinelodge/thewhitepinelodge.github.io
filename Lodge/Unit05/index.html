unit five

Unit Five is directly off the front porch. It is a single large room with a fireplace; two double beds; two swivel rockers; a circular table with four chairs; a toaster, a microwave, and a coffeepot; a small refrigerator; and a television with satellite access and VCR.

The bathroom has a shower. This unit sleeps four in relative comfort. In the summer, guests can relax in country comfort in rocking chairs on the front porch.
