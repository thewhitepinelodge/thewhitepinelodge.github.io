unit three

Unit Three is directly off the back deck of the lodge. It features three bedrooms: one with a double bed, one with a double and a twin, and a third with twin-sized bunk beds. There are also two twin-sized beds in the living room.

The living room has a television set, with satellite access and a VCR, and a harvest table that can open up to accommodate eight people. Unit Three also features its own thermostat and ample closet space.

There are two bathrooms. One has a shower, the other has a shower/tub combination. The kitchen includes a full-sized refrigerator with separate freezer; a full-sized stove; a microwave oven; a toaster; a coffeepot; and dishes, silverware, and glassware enough for ten people.
