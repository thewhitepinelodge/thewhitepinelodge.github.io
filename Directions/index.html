getting here

from route 91 in vermont

Take Exit 2 and watch for signs for Route 30 north. Approximately 30 miles after you get on Route 30, you will reach the hamlet of Rawsonville (the last village before this will be Jamaica). In Rawsonville, take Route 100 north to Route 11. At this junction, make a left onto Route 11. We are exactly 2.3 miles up on the left.

After dark or in case of snow, from Route 91 take Exit 6. Follow the signs to Route 103 north to Chester. In Chester, pick up Route 11 west. Stay on Route 11 through Londonderry. We are 2.3 miles from the Londonderry shopping center on your left.

from the taconic state parkway

Take the Taconic to 295 to Route 22. Take a right at the junction with NY 7, which will change into Route 9 in Vermont. In Bennington, take Route 7 north to Manchester (Exit 4). Make a right onto Route 11 heading east. We are about 11 miles down on your right.

from albany, new york

Take NY 7 which will change into Route 9 in Vermont. Then follow the directions in the paragraph above.

notes

As always, it helps to consult a map before you start a trip. It will give you a sense of distances between places.
