stay with us

Enjoy the privacy, warmth and comfort of your own apartment after a day exploring our beautiful Green Mountains. Relax in front of a crackling fire, snuggle up in an easy chair, or share a meal prepared in your own fully equipped kitchen. We supply linens, all the kitchen equipment, firewood, peace, and setting; you provide groceries and the desire to explore then relax. Units have satellite-access television, with VCR in the living room. Three have fireplaces; all have charm.

Our apartments are four-room or five-room suites and are perfect for families or large groups: the more the merrier. Well-behaved pets are welcome, too. For more information or to make a reservation, please follow the links at left.
