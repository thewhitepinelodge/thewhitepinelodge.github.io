our (winter) rates

At the White Pine Lodge, we have four apartments and one large room available. The rates quoted below are for double occupancy per night. Each additional occupant is $20 per night. Children 12 and under are $13 per night.

Our rates are subject to an additional 9% lodging tax. There are no additional charges or gratuities, except as noted below.

unit one: $195/night

This apartment sleeps four in two bedrooms, one more on a comfy sofa, and includes a fireplace in Colonial motif.

unit two: $180/night

This two-bedroom apartment sleeps seven, two on twin sofa-beds in living room. One flight up.

unit three: $185/night

This apartment has three bedrooms, two bathrooms, and a five-person minimum.

unit four: $185/night

This apartment is currently rented.

unit five: $150/night

This is a large room with two double beds and a fireplace. It sleeps four comfortably. Small fridge and microwave (no cooking); ground floor.

notes

Our rates are $10 per night higher for holiday periods.

We offer a 10% AAA discount.

There is no extra charge for infants with their own cribs; cribs are available for $7 per night. In a bed, infants pay the child rate of $13 per night.

You may bring pets if they are clean, quiet, and well-behaved. We prefer that they go with you on your adventures, but if they must be left behind, we'll let them out for you! The charge is $7 per pet per night.

We offer the following discounted rates applied Sunday through Thursday except during holiday periods:

Length of Stay Discount
5 nights 20% off
4 nights 15% off
3 nights 10% off
