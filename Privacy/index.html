our privacy policy

At the White Pine Lodge, we respect your privacy. To ensure that your personal data is always safe with us, we've established this Privacy Policy.

This policy applies to all information gathered on the White Pine Lodge website. If you would like to know what personal data we have on file for you, feel free to contact us. The relevant contact information can be found at the bottom of this and every site page.

information gathered

Other than aggregate information about total page views, etc., the only information we gather on the White Pine Lodge site is the information that you provide when you fill out our Making Reservations form.

how we use this information

Any information that you provide to us is used solely to provide you with the services you requested. We do not share this information with other agencies unless you ask us to in writing.

information security

Unless encrypted, email is not a secure medium. The forms on the White Pine Lodge web site use unencrypted email for delivery. Please do not send sensitive information (such as credit card numbers) to us via this web site.

use of cookies

Currently, the White Pine Lodge site does not use cookies. No personal information is stored on the server.
