house policy

Please read these policies carefully before making your reservation.

deposit

A deposit in the amount of 50% of the rental for the reserved period is required, with the balance to be paid upon arrival. Reservations will be held for five days while awaiting the deposit.

Before your arrival, your deposit applies to the beginning of the reserved period. After your arrival, your deposit applies to the end of your reserved period. Damages will be assessed if necessary.

cancellations

If you cancel your reservation more than 14 days before your scheduled arrival, we will refund your deposit, less a $35 cancellation fee. If you cancel your reservation with 14 days or less, your deposit will be forfeited. There will be no exceptions.

Note: The full daily rate will be charged if you arrive after your date specified or depart before the final date of your confirmed reservation.

check-in

You may check in any time after 3 PM. Check out is by 11 AM unless other arrangements are agreed upon in advance.
